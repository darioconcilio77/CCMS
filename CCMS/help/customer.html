Customers

D4P.CCMS.Customer Table 62000

The Customer is the top-level entity in the CCMS data hierarchy. Every Entra tenant and every BC environment belongs to a customer. Creating a customer record is the first data-entry step after completing the initial setup.

Customer fields

FieldTypeDescription
No. Code[20] Primary key. Assigned automatically from the number series configured in Setup, or entered manually.
Name Text[100] Descriptive name of the customer / partner organisation.
Number series
The Customer Number Series must be configured in Setup before creating the first customer record, otherwise BC will prompt you to enter the number manually.

Full card for a single customer. Includes the Customer FactBox on the right side, showing a count of associated tenants and environments at a glance.

How to create a customer

  1. Open Customers List from the Role Center or BC search.
  2. Click New to open a blank Customer Card.
  3. Enter the Name. The No. is auto-filled from the number series.
  4. Save (press Esc or navigate away). The record is created.
  5. Click Tenants to add the first Entra tenant for this customer.
